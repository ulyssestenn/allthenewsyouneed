All four sources were fetched directly this cycle. The WSJ (`feeds.content.dowjones.io`), NYT (`rss.nytimes.com`), and NBC (`feeds.nbcnews.com`) RSS endpoints returned successfully, and their article links are taken verbatim from those feeds.

No URLs were fabricated.
